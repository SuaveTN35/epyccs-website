## COMPLIANCE SECTION

### Section Headline:
## Compliance You Can Count On

### Content:
Healthcare delivery requires more than speed — it requires strict adherence to regulatory standards. EPYC Courier Service maintains full compliance with:

**HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)**
All drivers are HIPAA-trained and certified. Patient information is protected at every step of the delivery process.

**OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration)**
Our team follows OSHA bloodborne pathogen standards for safe handling of biological specimens and potentially infectious materials.

**Chain of Custody**
Complete documentation from pickup to delivery. Every handoff is recorded with timestamps, signatures, and temperature logs when required.

**Temperature Control**
Our vehicles are equipped for ambient, refrigerated (2-8°C), and frozen (-20°C) transport to maintain specimen and medication integrity.
