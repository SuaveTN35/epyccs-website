## What Makes Medical Courier Service Different?

### HIPAA Compliance Isn't Optional

Any courier handling protected health information (PHI) must maintain strict **HIPAA compliant** protocols. This includes:

- Secure chain-of-custody documentation
- Proper handling and temperature control for specimens
- Confidential transport of medical records
- Trained personnel who understand healthcare privacy requirements

Generic delivery services simply aren't equipped to meet these standards. A single compliance violation can result in significant fines and damage to your practice's reputation.

### Temperature-Controlled Transport

Many medical items require specific temperature ranges during transport. Blood samples, certain medications, and biological specimens can become useless—or even dangerous—if exposed to improper conditions. Professional medical couriers maintain appropriate environments throughout the delivery process.
