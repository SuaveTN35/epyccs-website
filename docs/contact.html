Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does EPYC Courier Service cover?

We serve all of Southern California including Los Angeles County, Orange County, San Diego County, and the Inland Empire (Riverside and San Bernardino Counties). Extended service to Ventura County is available upon request.

How fast can you deliver?

We offer multiple service levels: STAT (within 60 minutes), Rush (1-2 hours), Same-Day (within 4 hours), and Scheduled (at your specified time).

Are you available 24/7?

Yes! Our dispatch team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. Emergency and after-hours deliveries are always available.

Is EPYC HIPAA compliant?

Absolutely. Our medical courier drivers are fully trained in HIPAA and OSHA protocols. We maintain proper chain of custody documentation and temperature control for medical specimens and pharmaceuticals.

Do you offer real-time tracking?

Yes, every delivery includes GPS tracking. You'll receive real-time updates on your package location and ETA, plus proof of delivery with signature capture and photo confirmation.

How do I set up a corporate account?

Contact us at admin@epyccs.com or call (818) 217-0070 to discuss corporate account options. Benefits include volume discounts, streamlined billing, dedicated support, and priority dispatch.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), corporate accounts with invoicing, and cash for individual deliveries.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, EPYC Courier Service is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Your packages are protected from pickup to delivery.
