Our Medical Courier Services

Laboratory Specimen Transport

Secure transport of blood samples, urine specimens, tissue samples, and other biological materials. Temperature-controlled vehicles maintain specimen integrity from pickup to delivery.

  • Ambient, refrigerated, and frozen transport
  • Proper biohazard handling
  • Chain of custody documentation
  • Direct lab-to-lab delivery
  • STAT delivery within 60 minutes

Pharmaceutical Delivery

Safe, compliant delivery of prescription medications, controlled substances, and pharmacy supplies. Our drivers are trained in proper pharmaceutical handling procedures.

  • Controlled substance protocols
  • Temperature-sensitive medication transport
  • Pharmacy-to-patient delivery
  • Hospital pharmacy restocking
  • Clinical trial material transport

Medical Equipment & Supplies

Reliable delivery of medical devices, surgical supplies, and healthcare equipment. From small diagnostic tools to larger medical apparatus.
